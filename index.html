About Me

I am currently the Chief Scientist at Clustar. I received my Ph.D. from Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Hong Kong University of Science and Technology in 2019. Before that, I received my B.S. degree in Computer Science and Technology from University of Science and Technology of China in 2013.

Research Interests

My current interests are mainly about datacenter networks, RDMA networks and machine learning systems (with a special focus on federated machine learning).
